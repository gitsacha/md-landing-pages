## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Traditional urgent care is impersonal, crowded, and rushed. ERs have 4+ hour waits and $5,000+ bills. Patients feel like a number, not a person.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- ER: Insane wait times, astronomical bills, cold environment
- Chain urgent care (CityMD, etc.): Assembly line medicine, see a different doc every time, 5 minutes with the physician
- Concierge medicine: Expensive monthly retainers, not designed for acute/urgent needs
**What it costs them:** Hours of their day wasted, anxiety, delayed treatment, feeling dismissed
**Emotional tension:** "I'm sick and scared and I just want someone to take care of me properly"

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Other Beverly Hills urgent care clinics — lack the VIP/personalized touch, don't offer regenerative treatments
**Secondary:** Emergency rooms — too expensive, too slow, too impersonal
**Indirect:** Telehealth (Teladoc, etc.) — can't do physical exams, no procedures, no IV treatments
